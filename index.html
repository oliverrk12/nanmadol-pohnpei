Welcome to Nan Madol

The Venice of the Pacific

Nan Madol is an ancient archaeological site built on over 90 artificial islets connected by canals, located off the shore of Pohnpei in Micronesia. This website explores its mysteries, legends, and ongoing preservation efforts.
